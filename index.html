EnvironMentors is a year-long environmental research mentorship. UC Davis grad students, postdocs, and faculty work one-on-one with Sacramento-area high school students. Mentees design and run their own projects and present at the UC Davis Science Fair in spring.

How it works.

Each year we match Sacramento-area high school students with UC Davis researchers as mentors. Pairs meet every other week, scope a research project the student picks, and the student runs it through the school year.

In May, mentees present at the UC Davis Science Fair. A top cohort travels to the GCSE International Science Fair as a group, fully covered. Then summer's off.
