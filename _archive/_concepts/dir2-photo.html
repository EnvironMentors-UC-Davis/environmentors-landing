How it works

Three steps, one school year.

01

Get matched

You're paired one-on-one with a UC Davis grad student, postdoc, or faculty mentor.

02

Run your project

Pick a question that matters to you, then design and run the study across the year.

03

Present your science

Show your work at the UC Davis Science Fair. A top group goes to the GCSE International Fair.
