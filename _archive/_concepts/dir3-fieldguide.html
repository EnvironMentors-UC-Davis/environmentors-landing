How it works
[01]

Get matched

You're paired one-on-one with a UC Davis grad student, postdoc, or faculty mentor in environmental science.

[02]

Run your project

Pick a question that actually matters to you, then design and run the study across the school year.

[03]

Present your science

Show your work at the UC Davis Science Fair. A top group advances to the GCSE International Fair, fully covered.
