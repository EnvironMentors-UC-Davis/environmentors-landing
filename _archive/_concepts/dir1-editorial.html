How it works

01

Get matched

You're paired one-on-one with a UC Davis grad student, postdoc, or faculty mentor in environmental science.

02

Run your project

You pick a question that actually matters to you, then design and run the study across the year.

03

Present your science

Show your work at the UC Davis Science Fair. A top group goes on to the GCSE International Fair, fully covered.
