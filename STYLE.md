# Writing style — EnvironMentors landing site

The voice is **cool confidence**: not out of touch, not try-hard-kid, not salesman.
Confidence comes from specifics, not adjectives.

## Ground rules
1. **Talk to the reader, not about us.** Lead with what they'll *do*, not our mission statement.
2. **Short, declarative sentences.** No throat-clearing ("We are excited to announce...").
3. **Concrete over abstract.** "Design and run your own project," never "experiential learning opportunity."
4. **Ban the salesman words:** world-class, transformative, unlock, amazing, life-changing — and exclamation points.
5. **No emoji, no slang, no pandering.** Especially to the teenagers; respect their intelligence.
6. **Let the numbers brag.** 1:1, free, Sept–May, GCSE chapter, first-place finishes — state them flat.
7. **Read it aloud.** If it sounds like a brochure or a LinkedIn post, rewrite it.
8. **Active voice, second person** ("you") on the recruitment pages.

## Process
- **Ask, don't assume.** When phrasing matters (FAQ answers, taglines, post copy), bring Grey options
  rather than committing wording silently.
